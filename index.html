ObitoNet: Multimodal High-Resolution Point Cloud Reconstruction

Published on arXiv in Dec 2024

We present a transformer-based framework that fuses 2D image features (via a Vision Transformer) with 3D point-cloud geometry (via farthest point sampling and KNN tokenization). The key innovation is a cross-attention module that aligns and merges the semantic richness of the image data with the spatial precision of point-cloud tokens, enabling significantly improved reconstruction fidelity even under sparse or noisy 3D input. This fusion improves performance by roughly 18% over baseline models.
